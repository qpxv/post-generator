it was about what was happening in the world that day.

bitcoin crashes 40% and you're selling to crypto founders? that's your opener.

most people write cold outreach in a vacuum like the world doesn't exist outside their pitch.

the playbook:

- monitor big events in your prospect's world
- build outreach around the moment
- sound like someone who's paying attention

timing + relevance beats perfect copy every single time.

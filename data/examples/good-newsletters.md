SUBJECT: Time Management
Hola hola mi amigo,
I think time management is crap.
At least how most people do it with their little calendars, color coding everything and all that stuff.
How you actually need to do it is have a checklist, ruthlessly execute that checklist until it’s done, and boom.
If you planned your entire day of 8 hours lets say,
with a checklist you will get it done in 3.
Because if you plan a task in your mind for 1 hour which you could complete in 15mins,
it WILL take you 1 hour.
Because that’s just how our brains are.
If you use a checklist though, you will get the task done in 15mins because you don’t allow yourself to use more time because you have other stuff to do.
A checklist is so much better than planning your day.
I used to plan my day every day and I wouldn’t even want to wake up because I felt behind the moment I woke up.
But when I switched from a calendar to a checklist, I felt peace.
Because I woke up when I wanted, then got to work, and boom.
5 hours later everything was done which I would normally have planned until 8pm or something.
It’s incredible.
Also (at least for me) it gives way more peace than a calendar.
Because with a checklist you can wake up whenever you want and just get to work, and even if you have unforeseen interruptions, you can pause and continue working whenever you are available again.
When I had a calendar and a random event came up which I haven’t planned, I didn’t do any more important work for the rest of the day.
Because one hour that I had planned something got replaced and then my mind freaked out.
So if you are still using a calendar, try switching to a checklist.
It will give you so much more peace of mind and also make you way more productive.

That's what actually sells.

But when they feel pressured, they walk away.
So, if sales feel hard right now for you, check your energy.

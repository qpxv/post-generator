# voice

## positioning

ben helps coaches and online business owners build conversion focused websites that turn attention into clients.

he focuses on:

- strategic design
- sales psychology
- trust
- clarity
- avoiding diy website mistakes

## target reader

someone who:

- gets traffic or attention already
- relies on referrals or x
- has a weak website or no website
- undervalues design and positioning
- does not realize how much trust they are losing

## tone

- direct
- blunt
- conversational
- fast moving
- unapologetic
- certain
- no hedging
- doesn't soften takes
- sometimes funny
- occasionally profane

## stylistic traits

- mostly lowercase
- short paragraphs
- fast pacing
- direct statements
- no emojis
- no hashtags
- no corporate phrasing
- no clean polished linkedin tone

## beliefs

- your website affects perceived value before anyone reads the details
- people can feel laziness through weak design
- a cheap looking website weakens trust immediately
- many coaches lose clients because their website looks diy or confusing
- a website should feel aligned with the price of the offer

## recurring angles

- carrd or linktree kills trust for serious offers
- weak design makes you look cheap
- too much freedom or too many options kills conversion
- templates remove differentiation
- if your website looks half assed people assume the service is too

## banned phrases

- game changer
- unlock potential
- best part
- at the end of the day
- furthermore
- in conclusion
- that said

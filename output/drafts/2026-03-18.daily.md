## post 1

woke up this morning and the first thing i noticed was the magpies were already at their nest

before i had eaten anything

before i had done a single thing for the day

they were already building

and i just sat there watching them through the window doing my 9-5 stuff

and at some point this bird with a yellow beak and dotted brown feathers flew onto the table outside

grabbed a flower blossom

and just left

no hesitation

no second guessing

just saw what he wanted and took it

and i thought about how thats literally the whole game

not the grinding

not the suffering through shit you hate

just seeing the thing you want and moving toward it without making it complicated

because i spent most of today doing tasks that mean nothing to me

waiting for 4:45 so i could stop

counting down minutes

and the whole time the birds outside were just living

no countdown

no waiting to be released

just existing and doing what they do

and i know which one i wanna be

and i also know which one i currently am for 8 hours a day

and that gap is the most uncomfortable thing i have ever felt in my life

but at least i know its there

because if you cant feel that gap

you never close it

## post 2

built an ai system two days ago that reads my journal

writes posts that sound exactly like me

and pushes them automatically into scheduled slots

i just review them

takes me maybe 30 seconds

and i sat there today thinking about how insane it is that i could technically post 6 to 10 times a day now without it costing me any real time or energy

but the thing is i spent like an hour and a half just training it

feeding it my actual posts

writing a prompt that was so specific it took multiple attempts to get right

and now the output sounds genuinely like me

and people hear that and think okay nice a little ai tool

but thats not what happened

what happened is i treated the setup like the actual work

i cared about what went in

so what comes out is actually good

and this is the mistake i see everywhere

people spend 20 minutes on something and then wonder why the output is generic and useless

whether its an ai system

or a website

or a sales script

the lazy version gets lazy results

and the lazy version is always the one where you just plugged something in and hoped for the best

garbage in

garbage out

always

if you actually care about the output

you have to care about the input first

and most people skip that part

because the input is the unglamorous part

its not exciting to spend an hour writing a prompt

but its the difference between something that actually works and something that just technically exists

## post 3

had a call today that got cut short because my internet was being completely useless

even on hotspot

and i just sat there watching the loading spinner

and i thought about a physical therapy client i had a while back

got him 2.2k in three days

through his website alone

no outreach

no ads

no calls

just the website doing what a good website is supposed to do

and the reason i remembered that today is because i was frustrated

frustrated at things not working

at wasted time

at being stuck waiting on something outside my control

and that memory hit me like okay

this is exactly what a broken website feels like for a coach

they are out there putting in real effort

doing calls

posting content

running outreach

and then someone lands on their page

and the page just doesnt load right

or looks like it was made in 2014

or has no testimonials anywhere near the top

and the whole thing falls apart before they even get a chance

and the coach never sees it happen

they just wonder why their conversion rate is trash

while people visit the page

hit the loading spinner equivalent in their brain

and leave

the effort going in means nothing if the page is bleeding trust before the person even scrolls

because trust either gets built in the first few seconds

or it doesnt get built at all

## post 4

i had this realization today standing in the kitchen

i was thinking about what else i could automate with ai

and then my brain went okay but what should i never automate

and i landed on engaging with leads

immediately

without even thinking about it

because thats the one thing i swore i would never let a machine do for me

not because its hard

but because the second someone can feel that the reply is generated

you have lost them completely

and the thing about trust is it operates exactly like that

it lives in the details that feel human

the reply that shows you actually read their message

the testimonial that sounds like a real person talking and not a press release

the website that feels like a real human made it for a real human

and the second any of that feels automated or generic or copy pasted from a template

something in the other persons brain registers it

they cant always name it

they just feel it

and then they leave

coaches do this on their websites all the time

they write their own copy

and it reads like they asked chatgpt for ten reasons why someone should hire them

and it has zero personality

zero specificity

zero of the actual human that people would be paying money to work with

and then they wonder why people visit and dont book

its because nothing on the page felt like a real person

it felt like every other coach website

and when everything looks the same

people choose the one that doesnt

which is never yours if yours looks like the rest

## post 6

i was watching two pigeons fight each other outside today

just going at it

hitting each other with their wings

over what i can only assume was a spot at the bird feeder

and it made me think about something i see coaches do constantly on their websites

they try to fight everyone

they list every single thing they do

every service

every niche they can serve

every problem they have ever solved

because they are scared of losing someone

so they try to appeal to everyone

and the result is a website that means nothing to anyone

because when your headline is so broad it could apply to a thousand different people

it actually speaks to zero of them

the coaches who convert the best have websites that make a very specific person feel like the page was written for them specifically

not for the general category of person that maybe could possibly benefit from their work

but for the exact human they actually want to work with

and that feels like youre leaving people out

it feels like losing

like watching leads fly away because you didnt mention you could help them too

but the opposite is true

the more specific you are

the more the right person feels seen

and when someone feels seen

they book

they dont think about it

they just book

because nothing else they found online felt like it was talking to them directly

and yours did

the pigeons fighting over everything ended up with both of them getting nothing

pick your spot

and own it completely

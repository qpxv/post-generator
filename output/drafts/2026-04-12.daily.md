## post 2

a coach gets on a sales call

the prospect seemed warm in the dms

call goes well enough but they don't close

and the coach blames their offer or their price or their pitch

it was none of those things

the prospect googled them before the call

found a website that looked like it was made in 2019 by someone who had just discovered canva

and the trust was already gone before the camera turned on

your website is not a formality you fill out after you get clients

it is the thing that decides whether the call happens at all

people do not show up to sales calls neutral

they show up having already decided something about you

and that decision gets made in about four seconds on your homepage

## post 4

your website has one job before anything else

make the person reading it feel like they already know you can do what you say you can do

not inform them

not impress them with a long list of services

make them FEEL it

the coaches who convert cold traffic into clients are not the ones with the most credentials on their website

they are the ones whose website makes you feel stupid for even considering someone else

that feeling comes from design

it comes from the right words in the right order

it comes from social proof placed exactly where doubt lives

it does not come from a template you dragged and dropped at 2am

## post 6

a coach spends six months building an audience

posts every day

gets real engagement

people are warm

they click the link in the bio

and the website KILLS it

not kills it in a good way

kills the trust they built over six months in about three seconds

mismatched fonts

stock photos

a hero section that says "i help you reach your potential" and nothing else

the audience was real

the demand was real

the website turned it into nothing

what you post builds trust slowly over time

your website can destroy all of it instantly

that is the part people do not take seriously until they see the numbers

## post 1

noticed something today walking home from the bus

i felt weirdly self conscious about my hands

like i didnt know what to do with them when they were just hanging at my sides swinging normally

so i kept wanting to shove them in my pockets

and i caught myself doing it and thought wait

this is so stupid

my hands are just hanging there doing what hands do

and my brain is treating it like an emergency

so i made a rule for myself

no hands in pockets for the entire walk home

just let them hang

and it was uncomfortable as hell for the first five minutes

my brain kept screaming put them away nobody walks like this

and then after a while it just stopped

and i was just walking

normally

like a person

and this is the thing about confidence

it is not something you build by thinking about it

you build it by doing the uncomfortable thing until your brain stops treating it as a threat

you put your hands in your pockets because it feels safer

you dont post that content because it feels safer

you dont reach out to that lead because it feels safer

and every time you choose the pocket version of things

you are telling your brain that the normal thing is dangerous

that walking with your hands out is dangerous

that being seen is dangerous

so it gets worse

not better

the only way through it is to just do the uncomfortable version on purpose

hands out

post goes up

dm gets sent

and you just let your brain freak out until it gets bored and stops

because it always stops

it just needs enough evidence that nothing bad happened

and you have to be willing to look like an idiot for a little while to collect that evidence

## post 2

yanneck had like 20 comments on my merge request today

and my first reaction was that familiar thing where your stomach kind of drops

like okay here we go

but then i actually read through them

and it was mostly naming

component names

variable names

just the naming

and i sat there thinking i spent actual energy being nervous about that

not the logic

not the architecture

not whether the thing even works

the names

and this keeps happening to me in different areas

i get nervous before a call with yanneck and nothing bad happens

i overthink a dm before sending it and the person responds fine

i dread a code review and the feedback is about naming conventions

my brain consistently treats small things like they are career ending moments

and the gap between what i fear and what actually happens is insane every single time

i think this is why most people never do anything hard

not because hard things are actually that dangerous

but because their brain runs a preview of the worst version every single time

and they treat the preview like its guaranteed

so they avoid the call

avoid the post

avoid sending the dm

avoid submitting the work

because the preview felt so real

but the preview is not the event

the preview is just your brain protecting you from something it doesnt understand yet

and the only way to recalibrate it

is to do the thing

see what actually happens

and slowly build a database of okay nothing that bad occurred

it takes a while

but eventually your brain starts showing you more accurate previews

because you gave it real data instead of imagination

## post 3

there were like ten or thirteen magpies on the grass in the park today when i left work

just all doing their thing

picking at the ground

hopping around

completely unbothered

one of them crossed the walkway right in front of me and i stopped and just watched for a second

and then i got home and found something pink in the garden

some marshmallow packaging

and my mom said it was the wind

but the magpies were still sitting in the tree outside and they didnt fly away when i went out to pick it up

and usually they always fly away immediately

and i want to believe they left it there

which is probably insane

but then i thought about it

and isnt it kind of insane that i noticed it at all

a tiny pink thing on the grass

at a glance

from inside

and i cant explain that

i dont think its a coincidence

i think when you start paying attention to the world outside your head

actually paying attention

not just existing inside your thoughts while the world happens around you

things start catching your eye that you wouldve walked past a year ago

like your perception just gets sharper

not because anything changed out there

but because you trained yourself to actually look

most people walk through their whole life in their own head

earphones in

eyes on the phone

replaying conversations from three days ago

and the world is just happening around them but they are not in it

and i think that costs you more than you realize

not just the magpies

but the opportunity you walked past

the thing someone said that you missed because you were already preparing your response

the detail that wouldve changed everything if you had just been present enough to catch it

## post 4

i was nervous for a call with yanneck today

like actually nervous

heart doing that thing

and the call kept getting pushed because he was in meetings all morning

so i sat there at my desk from like 10am

just waiting

getting more in my head about literally nothing

and when we finally got on the call it was fine

completely fine

i told him where i was at he told me what to do next and we moved on

and this is the thing that kills me about myself sometimes

i have a solid track record of things being fine

like genuinely most things i dread are fine

and yet my brain refuses to update

it just keeps generating the same dread every single time like it has no memory

and coaches do this exact thing with their websites

they have a call booked with a potential client

and the lead goes to check their website before the call

and the website is some rushed thing they threw together two years ago

blurry logo

generic headline that says something like welcome to my coaching practice

no testimonials

no case studies

colors that dont match

and the lead is sitting there on the other end trying to feel confident about this person

trying to find the thing that tells them this is legit

this person knows what they are doing

and it is not there

so they get on the call already half gone

already a little unsure

and now the coach has to spend the first ten minutes of the call basically undoing the damage their own website did

instead of walking in already trusted

already validated

because the website did that work before the call even started

your website is the thing people check when you are not in the room

and if it looks like you do not take yourself seriously

they will not take you seriously either

no matter how good the call goes

## post 5

my arms are completely cooked right now

i literally cannot extend them fully

tried to close a protein powder container with one hand and had to use both

opened my window and it taxed my triceps

this is the most fatigued my arms have ever been from a workout and i feel genuinely incredible

and the weird thing is i was also doing outreach at the same time

alternating between sets and leads

and i was more locked in during the outreach than i normally am when i just sit down and try to focus on it

like the physical exhaustion was somehow making my brain quieter

no overthinking

just type the message

lift the weight

type the message

lift the weight

and i think there is something real there

your brain generates a lot of noise when it has too much space

when you are just sitting there trying to focus it finds a thousand reasons to wander

you start thinking about whether the message sounds weird

whether the person will ignore you

whether you are even doing this right

but when your body is also working

when your arms are burning and your whole system is occupied

the noise just goes away

and all that is left is the thing in front of you

i dont think this works for everyone

but for me sitting still and trying to force focus is the hardest version

moving and working at the same time is somehow easier

which is so backwards that i almost did not believe it until tonight proved it

## post 6

walked past the park at the end of the day and the birds were going absolutely insane

it had just rained and everything smelled different

that specific smell after rain hits and it is like your brain resets for a second

i stood there at the schienen just listening

and i thought about how i genuinely used to not care about any of this

like nature was just background noise

something that existed behind whatever was on my phone

and now i stop for it

i notice the magpies

i notice the smell

i notice when the air feels like a storm is coming

and i got home and built my whole evening around that energy somehow

weights done

outreach done

posts scheduled

all of it

and i think the attention thing is connected

because a coach who actually pays attention to what their client is experiencing before they even get on a call

is the kind of coach who builds a website that speaks to that person before they ever reach out

not a generic headline that says something like transform your life

but the specific line that makes someone think how did they know that was exactly what i was feeling

that only comes from paying attention

from actually caring what it is like to be the person on the other side

most coaches write their website from their own perspective

what they do

what they offer

their method

their story

and the person reading it is sitting there thinking but what about me

the coaches who convert

the ones where people read the website and immediately book a call

they wrote it from the outside in

they started with the experience of the person arriving

not with themselves

and that is not a design thing or a copywriting trick

it is just paying attention

the same way i started paying attention to birds

and suddenly noticed things i used to walk straight past

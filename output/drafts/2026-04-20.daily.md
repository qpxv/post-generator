## post 2

the guy sending me chatgpt screenshots to kick off a 12k project

bro is not ready

and i don't mean that as a dig i genuinely mean the people who treat ai as a replacement for thinking haven't figured out what they're actually selling yet

a coach with a bad website has the same problem

they haven't figured out what they're selling

so the website becomes a collection of vibes and stock photos and a button that says "work with me"

and visitors feel that immediately

not consciously

but they land on it and something feels off and they leave

trust is not built by listing your credentials

it's built by the feeling someone gets in the first 4 seconds

that feeling either says "this person knows what they're doing"

or it says "i'm not sure"

and NOBODY books a call when they're not sure

## post 3

coaches are losing clients to people who are worse at coaching than them

happens every day

the better coach has a mediocre website

the worse coach invested in how they present

and the prospect who doesn't know either of them yet makes a decision based on what they can actually see

this is not unfair

this is just how trust works before someone knows you

your website is the first impression and in most cases it's the only impression you get to make

if it looks like you built it yourself on a free plan in 2021

that's the version of you they're evaluating

not your actual skills

not your results

not how good you are in a session

just that website

## post 6

a coach once told me their website doesn't matter because they get all their clients from referrals

six months later they asked me why new people kept landing on their page and ghosting

referrals don't need your website to trust you

strangers do

and the moment you want to grow beyond the people who already know you

your website stops being optional

it becomes the thing that either closes the gap between stranger and client

or kills the chance entirely

most coaches find this out too late

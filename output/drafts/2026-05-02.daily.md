## post 3

i built a bridge in minecraft tonight and i'm unreasonably proud of it

which made me realize something about design

nobody tells you when something looks right

you just feel it

the proportions are good, the structure makes sense, it fits the environment around it

and that feeling is exactly what visitors get when they land on a website that was actually built with intention

they don't think "great font choice" or "smart layout"

they just feel like they're in the right place

like this person is legit

like they can trust what they're reading

and when that feeling is missing

when the website looks like someone was just moving things around hoping it would click

visitors feel THAT too

they can't name it but they leave

trust is a feeling before it's a decision

and your website is either creating it or killing it in the first 5 seconds

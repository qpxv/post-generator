## post 1

smell is the most underrated first impression signal

everyone talks about looks

nobody talks about smell

if someone looks decent but smells incredible you want to be around them

if someone looks incredible but smells bad you want to leave

your website works the same way

the visual design is the smell

people cannot explain why they trust certain websites and not others

they just feel it instantly

something feels right or it does not

that feeling is design doing its job invisibly

when design is bad people cannot point to what's wrong

they just feel uneasy and click away

when design is right they feel safe and they stay

you are not just designing a website

you are ENGINEERING a feeling

and that is an entirely different skillset than moving around rectangles

## post 2

watched two kids at the bus stop today

brothers

trolling each other about the time on the display

talking about mentos and cola explosions

one of them dropped trash and picked it up without being told

you can tell immediately when kids are raised well

it reads in every small thing they do

your website is the same

the little things are what give it away

the spacing that feels intentional

the font that does not fight itself

the color that does not scream

the layout that guides without pushing

none of these things are what visitors consciously notice

but they add up to a feeling of being in good hands

or a feeling of something being slightly off

coaches spend months perfecting their messaging

and then put it on a website that subconsciously tells people the opposite

the details are not decoration

they are the whole argument

## post 3

600 calories yesterday and today my brain was completely offline

could not focus

could not process anything

dragging through the whole day on empty

and it made me think about how many coaches are running their entire business on a website that is doing the same thing to their visitors

not broken

not offensive

just not giving the brain what it needs to feel confident

no clear headline

no obvious next step

information everywhere with no hierarchy

the visitor arrives and their brain immediately has to work harder than it should

and a confused brain does not buy

it leaves

your website should be doing cognitive work FOR your visitors not assigning it to them

the moment they land they should feel oriented

they should know who you are what you do and what to do next

within seconds

not after scrolling

not after reading three paragraphs

SECONDS

if your website makes people think too hard you have already lost them

## post 4

there was a violin player in the tunnel today

same guy i saw in february

same spot

same sound

and something about it just hit different because i recognized him

familiarity does something to people that novelty cannot

and coaches keep trying to impress visitors with novelty

new layout every few months

totally different color palette

new photos

new copy

when the thing that actually converts is the opposite

consistency

showing up the same way every time someone lands on your website

same voice

same visual identity

same clear offer

people do not hire coaches they just met

they hire coaches they feel like they already know

your website is not a one-time pitch

it is the place people come back to

and every time it looks different or feels off brand they have to start the trust process from zero again

stop redesigning your way out of conversions

build something consistent and let familiarity do the work

## post 6

a raven found two breadcrumbs at the traffic light this morning

grabbed one and flew off immediately

did not stand there weighing his options

did not look around for a better crumb somewhere else

saw what he needed and moved

most coaches i talk to have the opposite problem

their website has eleven things competing for attention

a pop-up

a blog

a podcast

a free guide

an about page that reads like a biography

a services page with four different offers

and somewhere buried in all of it is the one thing they actually want people to do

the raven knew what he came for

your website should too

one offer

one clear path

one reason to stay

everything else is noise and noise COSTS you the sale before the conversation even starts

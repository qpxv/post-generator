## post 1

i walked into the office this morning and could tell exactly what mood jasmin was in before she even finished saying good morning

just from the energy of the two words

and that made me think about how your website does the exact same thing

before anyone reads a single word of copy they already have a feeling

the design is saying good morning first

and most coaches have no idea what their website is saying

the font is saying "i threw this together in a weekend"

the colors are saying "i copied someone else"

the layout is saying "i don't really take this seriously"

and the visitor already knows

they haven't read anything yet but the vibe is there

you CANNOT outwrite a bad first impression

the copy doesn't matter if the design already said the wrong thing

your website opens its mouth before you do

make sure it says the right thing

## post 2

the professor at uni has his entire life stored in a database

every movie he's ever watched logged and organized

because he's so obsessed with databases he accidentally taught me things nobody else in the class knows

and that's actually how clients think about the coaches they hire

they don't want a generalist who knows a little about everything

they want the person who is so clearly obsessed with one thing that the obsession leaked into everything around them

your website should look like that

it should look like someone who cannot stop thinking about solving this one specific problem for this one specific type of person

most coach websites look like they were built to impress everyone

and they impress nobody

niche the obsession down until it's obvious

## post 3

failed the exam before even walking in

thought i was going to fail it anyway

then sat down wrote everything out clean got almost every question answered and left feeling good

the thing that was blocking me wasn't the material

it was the story i had been running that i wasn't ready

the prep was done the knowledge was there

but i had already decided the outcome before the test started

coaches do this with their websites all the time

"i'll fix it when i have more clients"

"it's fine for now"

"people hire me based on referrals anyway"

the website is already failing clients before they even land on it

and the coach already decided that's acceptable

the PREP is done you know what you do you get results for people

the only thing failing is the story that your website doesn't matter
